Trillium Regional Fraternity
 

The official Newsletter of the Secular Franciscan Order, Trillium Region, is the 

Good News Report 

We aim to publish in the middle to the end of September, December,
 March and June.

Through the vehicle of this newsletter the Minister and members of the Trillium Council hope to keep all Secular Franciscans in the Region aware of activities promoted by the Region, and of new information coming from the National and International Fraternities. The TRF council also hopes to promote a greater awareness of our Franciscan richness by inter-fraternity sharing. And don't forget the Joke Corner!

Usually included are articles from the Minister, Spiritual Assistant, Youth Coordinator, Formation Director and often other members of the Trillium Regional Fraternity. Articles of interest such as the history of the San Damiano Cross, the Tau, its meaning and origin; Justice, Peace and the Integrity of Creation are also included. 
Sometimes there will be items of National and International news.

A Local Fraternity Bulletin Board page tells of such items as the latest Elections, Obituaries and of course Professions. Especially, for this page, any items you wish to include are warmly welcomed and will be published space permitting; some editing may be necessary. Articles to be sent by the 8th of the month of publishing, 

To: Newsletter Editor - Maurice Prindiville, ofs; 
4 Pioneer Crt., St Catharines,  ON.  L2N 7B6
